1. The Rising Of The Shield Hero

The Rising of the Shield Hero is an anime about a gamer, Naofumi Iwatani, who is summoned to a fantasy world along with three other heroes to fight monsters during "Waves" of destruction. However, Naofumi is quickly betrayed by his party and falsely accused of a crime, leaving him isolated and distrusted by the kingdom. With only a shield and a hatred for the world, he must find a way to survive and protect the kingdom that despises him.

2. Dororo

Dororo is about a young man named Hyakkimaru who travels 16th-century Japan on a quest to defeat demons and reclaim the body parts stolen from him at birth by his power-hungry father. He is joined by a young orphan thief named Dororo, and together they battle supernatural foes to restore Hyakkimaru's body, while also exploring themes of humanity, identity, and morality.

4. The Apothecary Diaries

The Apothecary Diaries is a mystery-drama anime series about a young woman named Maomao who was kidnapped from her home in the red-light district and sold into servitude in the imperial palace. Despite trying to keep a low profile, her skills as an apothecary lead her to solve various medical and political mysteries within the court, catching the attention of the high-ranking eunuch Jinshi. The show is set in a fictional country inspired by Imperial China and blends elements of mystery, historical fiction, and medical drama.
