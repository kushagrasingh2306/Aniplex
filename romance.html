1. A Silent Voice

A Silent Voice is an anime film about a former bully named Shoya who tries to atone for his past by reconnecting with the deaf girl, Shoko, he used to torment in elementary school. The story explores themes of bullying, guilt, redemption, mental health, and the challenges of communication and social anxiety.

2. Your Name

Your Name is a 2016 anime film about two teenagers, Mitsuha and Taki, who mysteriously swap bodies. Mitsuha, a rural high school girl, dreams of living in Tokyo, while Taki is a city boy with aspirations to become an architect. Their body-swapping connection intensifies, forcing them to find a way to meet and grapple with the strange and emotional consequences that result from their intertwined fates, especially after a comet alters their reality.

3. I Want To Eat Your Pancreas

I Want to Eat Your Pancreas is a Japanese animated film about a nameless high school bookworm who discovers his popular classmate, Sakura Yamauchi, is secretly dying from a terminal pancreatic disease. He finds her journal, and their shared secret leads them to form an unlikely but deep friendship as they embark on a journey to live life to the fullest by checking items off her bucket list before she passes away.

4. 5 Centimeters Per Second

5 Centimeters per Second is a 2007 anime film by Makoto Shinkai that follows the story of two childhood friends, Takaki and Akari, and their long-distance relationship as they grow up. It's an emotional, three-part drama about young love, missed connections, and the slow drifting apart of people over time, depicted with breathtaking visuals. The title refers to the speed at which cherry blossom petals fall, symbolizing how people slowly drift apart.
